Splash Book will support your improvement.

  • All your games

    You can create a scorebook according to the type of practice, tournament, league and so on. It is also possible to manage multiple game sheets in each book.

  • Recorded visually

    It is possible to visually record killed pins, remaining placement, hit course. You can also shoot movies together, you can objectively look back at the bowling.

  • Analyze your bowling

    Game results are aggregated for each user and statistical values are displayed. Analysis of improvement points is possible by trends according to lane condition and video.
